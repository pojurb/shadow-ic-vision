# DEC-0002 - Approve Codex Protocol V3 Vision

Status: `accepted`

Date proposed: 2026-07-02

Approving authority: user

## Context

`VISION.md` must pass the vision gate before product strategy, milestone design,
evaluation architecture, technology selection, or product implementation may
begin.

The revised vision now defines:

- a behaviorally bounded initial target user
- the core problem as lost reasoning and unreviewed assumptions rather than
  market-timing performance
- a product promise based on cited evidence and explicit uncertainty
- achievable trust, privacy, and provider-disclosure commitments
- human decision authority and explicit non-goals
- Horizon 1 as a wedge delivered through multiple vertical slices
- product metrics that do not reward trading, alert volume, low correction
  counts, or rushed decisions

## Decision Requested

Approve `VISION.md` as the product north star and boundary document for Codex
Protocol V3.

Approval authorizes the product-strategy gate only. It does not approve a
milestone, architecture, stack, dependency, schema, or product implementation.

## Options

1. Accept the revised vision and begin product strategy.
2. Request targeted changes while keeping this decision proposed.
3. Reject the direction and revise the product premise.

- `docs/PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md` may move from `not_started` to `in_progress`.
- All implementation and technology decisions remain gated until their later
